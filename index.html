Haoji Zhang (张颢继)

I am a second-year master's student at Shenzhen International Graduate School, Tsinghua University, majoring in Data Science and Information Technology. I expect to graduate in 2027. I am fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Yansong Tang in IVG@SZ group.

Before that, I received my B.S. degree in Mathematics and Physics and B.E. degree in Materials Science and Engineering from Tsinghua University in 2024.

I feel honored to have internships in Prof. Jun Zhu's TSAIL Lab, Prof. Gao Huang's LEAP Lab, ByteDance Seed / Intelligent Creation, working with amazing mentors such as Dr. Yulin Wang, Prof. Xiaojie Jin, Dr. Jiashi Feng, Dr. Longyin Wen.

My research focuses on building efficient and powerful MLLMs , including:

  • Efficient Long Video Understanding: AR, VQA, VTG, STVG
  • Large-scale MLLM Training: PE, PT, SFT, Post-Train (GRPO, Tool-RL)
  • Agentic Model and Multimodal Reasoning
  • Unified Understanding and Generation Model
